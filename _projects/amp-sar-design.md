Dissecting how charge, hydrophobicity, amphipathicity, and helicity shape the activity, selectivity, and stability of cationic antimicrobial peptides, and using those rules to redesign natural toxins (decoralin, polybia-CP, VmCT1, mastoparans) into potent, non-toxic synthetic therapeutics.
